About Hotel


Tradition since 1999

The Palace of Food was founded in Jaisalmer by Mr. Nimesh Thakur & Mr. Siddharth Worlikar with the ambition to establish amazing hotel, where people can enjoy multi-cuisine food, spend quality time with family, friends creating beautiful memories. We only use seasonal ingredients. Hotel has been credited as 5-star hotel by national htoel rating system of India. As Mr. Hemingway once wrote in Times of India, "When I think of having delicious food, the action always takes place in the Palace of Food. "

Whether it is a birthday party, anniversaries, or corporate meetings,dinner nights, The Palace of food has you covered. You don't have to look far for a birthday party hall in Jaisalmer since we have all the facilities that can make your celebrations memorable. With one of the best hotel in Jaisalmer, bring in your team,friends and family whether it is for seminars, annual events of just for recreational purposes.
